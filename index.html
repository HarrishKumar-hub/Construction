How We Build Your Home

A transparent, step-by-step process from first meeting to handing over the keys.

1. Free Consultation

We start with a friendly meeting to understand your family's needs, plot size, and budget expectations.

2. Planning & Design

Our engineers draft a Vastu-compliant floor plan and elevation design tailored exactly to your plot.

3. Approval & Agreement

We present a transparent cost breakdown. Once approved, we sign a contract with a clear timeline.

4. Construction Phase

Foundation to roof — we use premium materials and our skilled team manages every detail of the build.

5. Finishing & Handover

Final plastering, tiling, electrical, and painting — then we hand over your complete, move-in-ready dream home. 🏠
